IC17

The NYU Steinhardt Programs in Music Education and Music Technology in association with IMPACT Workshops presents the fourth IMPACT Conference August 2 - August 5, 2017 in New York City.

This conference will bring together school, community arts, media, music educators and activists, musicians and artists, health professionals, university researchers, and industry together for a 3-day series of interactive sessions, performances, and hands-on workshops.

All submissions will be reviewed by a panel of researchers and practitioners in education, the arts, and technology. Notifications will be made by March 15, 2017. Early submissions are encouraged.


The four strands of this year’s conference are:

• Music, Health, and Accessibility

• Interactive Multimedia Experiences

• Experience Design for Learning and Creating

• Social Justice and Activism

Conference presentations take the form of 1 hour hands-on demo sessions and 2 hour interactive workshops.

Submission Deadline

The deadline for conference submissions is by midnight on March 1, 2017. All submissions will be reviewed by an expert panel of reviewers. Notifications will be made by March 15, 2017. Early submissions are encouraged.

Presentation Formats

Presenters will have the option of submitting proposals for 1 hour hands-on demo sessions or 2 hour interactive workshops. The point of these sessions is to actively involve the audience as participants, rather than lecture and direct presentation.

Presentation Requirements

All presenters must register to attend the symposium by the pre-registration deadline so their names will be included in the program.
